The Experience

Steak, Seafood & the ultimate VIBE dining experience

STK Chicago brings the ultimte vibe dining experience to the River North neighborhood of Chicago. STK artfully blends the modern steakhouse and a chic lounge into one, offering a dynamic fine dining experience with the superior quality of a traditional steakhouse.

In the Neighborhood

In Chicago’s River North, STK Chicago sits on West Kinzie Street surrounded by buzzing bars, galleries, and some of the city’s most talked-about steakhouses.

Start the night with riverfront strolls or cocktails nearby, then sink into our sleek dining room for signature STK steaks, seafood towers, bold cocktails, and a DJ-driven vibe that feels more like a night out than a traditional chop house.

Weekend plans in Chicago? Book Sunday Brunch or Steak Night, explore the Riverwalk or nearby theater and nightlife in the Loop, then circle back to STK Chicago for $3 | $6 | $9 Happy Hour, late-night bites, and full-scale VIBE dining.

FAQs

Where is STK Chicago located?

STK Chicago is located at 9 W Kinzie St, Chicago, IL 60654, in the River North neighborhood.

Does STK Chicago have Happy Hour?

Yes, STK Chicago offers a $3 | $6 | $9 Happy Hour on select days; see the Happy Hour Menu or Happenings section for specific days and times.

Does STK Chicago offer brunch?

STK Chicago serves Sunday Brunch, between 10am and 2pm, with modern steakhouse dishes, brunch classics, and cocktails in a high-energy River North setting.

Can I host a private event at STK Chicago?

Yes. STK Chicago offers some of River North’s hottest private dining rooms, accommodating from 15 up to 500 guests; contact [email protected] or the listed events number to plan your celebration or corporate event.

Is there parking near STK Chicago?

Guests can use nearby River North garages and street parking around Kinzie Street; check local signage or call the restaurant for the latest parking recommendations.
